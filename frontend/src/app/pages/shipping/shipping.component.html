Knutsford Express Delivery

We offer delivery through Knutsford Express. The delivery fee is $300 JMD.

  • Fee: $300 JMD
  • Pickup: Customers collect their packages at a Knutsford location and pay the applicable package fee.
  • Best for: Customers outside our local cash-on-delivery zones.
Note: Once your package has been sent, we will share tracking or pickup details when available.
