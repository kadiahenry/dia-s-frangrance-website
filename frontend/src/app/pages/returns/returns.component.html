Quick Summary

We accept returns for items that were delivered in person. No returns are accepted for items that were shipped via Knutsford or the Post Office.

Last updated: March 3, 2026

✅

Return Eligibility

  • Items delivered in person: eligible for return.
  • Items shipped by Knutsford or the Post Office: not eligible for return.
📦

How Returns Work

  1. If our bearer arrives with your order and you decide not to keep the item, the delivery fee will still be charged.
  2. You may return the item at the time of delivery if you are not satisfied.
  3. If you would prefer a different product, we can arrange an exchange.
  4. If you do not want a replacement item, you will receive a refund on the spot.

Tip: Keep your receipt or order confirmation to speed things up.

💳

Refunds

Refunds are issued immediately once the item is returned at delivery and confirmed.
