What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember useful information such as your session, preferences, and browsing choices.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar storage technologies to:

  • Keep core site features working properly
  • Remember shopping and account-related preferences
  • Store cookie consent choices
  • Improve the site experience when optional cookies are enabled in the future

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These are required for the website to function correctly. They help with essential features such as navigation, sign-in state, secure account-related actions, and cart persistence.

Preference Cookies

These remember useful choices such as cookie consent preferences and other site settings designed to improve your experience.

Optional Cookies

Optional cookies may include analytics or similar tools that help us understand how visitors use the site. We will ask for your permission before enabling non-essential cookies.

How to Manage Cookies

You can choose whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies from the consent banner shown on the site. You can reopen the cookie settings at any time from this page or the footer, and you can also manage cookies through your browser settings.
